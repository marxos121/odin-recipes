Cottage pie

Description

A scrumptuous cottage pie with hearty beef and crumbly potatoes

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1.5kg potatoes
  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • Gravy capsules
  • Peas
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil of choice

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes, rinse and put start boiling; salt to taste
  2. Start preparing beef, preferably using our delicious taco meat recipe
  3. Once the potatoes are soft throughout, drain the water, add cream and mash
  4. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  5. Place fried meat on a baking tray and spread evenly
  6. Drain and add peas
  7. Prepare a cup of gravy according to instructions on the packaging and pour it on top of meat
  8. Add and spread the potates
  9. Place the tray in the oven
  10. Bake for 40 minutes or until the top of the potatoes turns light-brown
