Taco meat

Description

Delicious fried ground beef you can use for taco, burrito or spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • 500g of raw ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil (preferably lard, tallow or olive oil)

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Add oil of your choice and let it get hot
  3. Place meat on the pan
  4. After around 1 minute, break up the meat into 4 parts
  5. Continue frying and breaking up the meat gradually
  6. Once meat is broken up into relatively small pieces, add salt and pepper
  7. Continue fryying until brown
