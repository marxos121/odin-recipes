Scrambled eggs

Description

Delightful scrambled eggs, perfect to kickstart your day with a healthy breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Lard (you may use other oil but lard is strongly advised!
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix a desired number of eggs in a bowl
  2. Salt to taste
  3. Heat up a pan with the oil over small heat
  4. Empty the bowl onto the pan
  5. Fry slowly until the eggs reach a satisfactory texture, stirring every 15 seconds
